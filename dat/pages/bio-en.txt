<div class="ff-bellota">

**Hebe Argentieri** is a jewelry artist based in Junín, Argentina, who works on wearable pieces exploring her relationship with the territory. Materials are central to her discourse; she seeks to transform them to unveil hidden layers and compose narratives connected to her environment through her feminist lens.

Her practice is nourished by environmental sensitivity, driving her to rethink matter by repurposing discarded plastics and developing biomaterials.

Since 2015, she has taught classes at her own workshop in Junín.

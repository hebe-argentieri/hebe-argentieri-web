**Hebe Argentieri** is a jewelry artist based in Junín, Argentina whose work results from the influence of territory and the exploration of materials.

She is part of the collectives [ [[= G.cfg.joyeros_argentinos.name ]] ]([[= G.cfg.joyeros_argentinos.url ]]), [ [[= G.cfg.caracu.name ]] ]([[= G.cfg.caracu.url ]]), Km0, [ [[= G.cfg.totoral.name ]] ]([[= G.cfg.totoral.url ]]).

Since 2015 she has taught jewelry design at her own studio as well as online seminars.

<small>She received the Creation Grant from the National Endowment for the Arts to develop her work on biomaterials.</small>
